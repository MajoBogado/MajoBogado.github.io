Software Developer

Ten years of full-stack development across backend (C#, SQL) and frontend (React, Angular, Aurelia, XAML), working in clinical trials, fintech, and consumer apps at companies like Accenture, Aerolab, Belatrix (now part of Globant), Hexacta (now part of GlobalLogic), and Lagash Systems (now part of MercadoLibre).

I liked building things, trying new technologies, and solving problems with code. As I got deeper into design, my focus naturally shifted toward the UI, which is why the later years of my dev career leaned more frontend.

I still enjoy coding here and there, and having that background is genuinely valuable to me today. It lets me have richer, more grounded conversations across disciplines (with engineering, with design, with product) without losing sight of the bigger picture. It also means I can keep an eye on implementations and speak the language of trade-offs in a way that's actually useful.

More than anything, knowing how to code means I can just… make things. It feeds every project I work on, and you can probably see that across this site. It's one more tool in the toolbox, and a pretty versatile one. I can put something together quickly when needed, then hand it off to a dedicated developer to improve it. 😉
