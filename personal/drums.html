Drums

I'm learning — very much a beginner. If you're curious how, I've been following online lessons on Musora. have you heard of Drumeo on YouTube? They have an app called Musora where they upload classes and videos of great drummers teaching you how to play their songs. They also have what they call "the method", nothing but a series of foundational lessons for beginners like me.

Not much more to share for now, just know it's one of my interests and something I'm actually doing.

Oh, Musora also has guitar, piano, and bass. They're not sponsoring me btw 😅, I just genuinely think it's a good app so far, in case you want to check it out.
